Festival & Exhibition В· December 2025

Earth Body

EARTH BODY Art Exhibition: 4вҖ“30 December 2025

Opening: 6/12 at 20:00 @ Calibra Votanikos

Calibra and Cube Noir present the EARTH BODY exhibition, an artistic project exploring the relationship between the human being and the body as material and space.

We explore the earthly nature of the body and the multiple forms our existence can take when it touches soil, water, air and fire. Artists from different fields вҖ” visual arts, painting, ceramics, video art, photography and performance art вҖ” co-create a living space where the body becomes an earthly archive, memory, narrative and resistance.

An experience to be lived.

The performance В«AbundanceВ» is presented within the EARTH BODY exhibition, alongside the sculpture show.

Curator: Sofia Stavrakaki
