Festival · Bageion Hotel

Περάσματα ΙΙ

A contemporary performance art festival held in the historic Bageion Hotel, Omonoia — an abandoned building turned into a living space of artistic encounters. Performance, dance, sound installations, and live actions unfold simultaneously across floors and rooms.

The second edition unfolded as a 12-hour overnight event, 17:00–05:00, with performances appearing in sudden bursts or slowly unfolding for hours. Visitors moved freely through the building, composing their own paths through the work.
