First selection — Arte Laguna Prize 2025

The performance work «Abundance» was selected in the First Selection phase of the 20th edition of the Arte Laguna Prize.

The Arte Laguna Prize is an internationally recognised contemporary art institution based in Venice. This selection places the work among the artistic proposals that stood out from international submissions and advanced to the second evaluation phase.

Full details and the complete First Selection list are available on the official prize website.
