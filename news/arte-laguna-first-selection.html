First selection — Arte Laguna Prize 2025

Abundance has been selected for the first selection of the 20th Arte Laguna Prize.

The 20th edition of the Arte Laguna Prize — one of the most established international contemporary art competitions, based in Venice — announced its first selection in 2025. Abundance, a performance work examining mass consumption and the erosion of identity, is among the works selected.

Full details and the complete first-selection list are available on the prize's official site.
