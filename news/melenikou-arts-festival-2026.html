Melenikou Arts Festival 2026

«Bodies in Suspension» — 8 Performance Art Works over three evenings, as part of the Melenikou Arts Festival 2026.

The Melenikou Arts Festival 2026 (3rd Year) takes place from 29 May to 5 July 2026, dedicated to theatre, dance and performance art, at Calibra Votanikos and Cube Noir Votanikos.

As part of the festival, the three-day programme «Bodies in Suspension» is presented, 19 – 20 – 21 June at 20:30, with 8 live performance art works.

Desire is suspended. Decision is suspended. The life we haven’t lived is suspended.
