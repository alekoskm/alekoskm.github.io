ATHENSHOT Festival — Abundance

The performance "Abundance" is presented at the ATHENSHOT Festival — Saturday 4 July, 19:00.

The ATHENSHOT Festival returns, bringing together artists, creators, and the public in a major celebration of contemporary art, photography, performance, and creative expression. With a multifaceted program of actions, exhibitions, and live presentations, the festival serves as a meeting point for those who love art and culture.

As part of the festival, on Saturday 4 July at 19:00, the performance "Abundance" will be presented, a short artistic action that approaches the concept of abundance through live expression and interaction with the public.

The ATHENSHOT Festival provides space for new ideas, innovative approaches, and contemporary forms of artistic creation, offering visitors a unique experience that combines different arts and creative minds.

We invite you to visit the festival and witness its activities and presentations firsthand, in an environment that highlights contemporary artistic creation and culture.
