Exhibition · 2025

Arte Laguna Prize — 20th Edition

Venice · First selection

Abundance presented within the framework of the 20th Arte Laguna Prize.

The Arte Laguna Prize is one of the most established international contemporary art competitions, hosted in Venice and now in its 20th edition. The first selection of 2025 brings together a broad cross-section of contemporary practice across painting, sculpture, photography, performance, and digital media.

Abundance — a performance work examining mass consumption, the erosion of identity, and the body as a site of excess — is among the works in this selection.
