Practice & biography

Alexandros Mylonas was born in Messolonghi in 1985 and lives and works in Athens. He studied Computer Engineering and serves in the Hellenic Air Force.

Since 2014, his practice has moved through acting — across theatre, film, and television — guided by a sustained interest in human expression and the body in performance. Since 2024, he has dedicated himself to the study and practice of performance art under the mentorship of visual artist Sofia Stavrakakis.

His work brings together physical presence, sculpture, and ritual to interrogate questions of collective identity, internal excavation, and transformation.
