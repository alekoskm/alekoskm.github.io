Bio

Alexandros Mylonas was born in Messolonghi in 1985. He lives and works in Athens. He studied Computer Engineering and serves as a military officer in the Hellenic Air Force. He has been actively involved in acting since 2014, having participated in theatre productions, films and television series in supporting roles, guided by his love for theatre and human expression.

He has attended numerous acting workshops and since 2024 has been focusing on the study and practice of Performance Art, sculpture and installation, under the guidance of visual artist Sofia Stavrakaki.
