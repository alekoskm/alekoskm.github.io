Abundance Αφθονία

In a ceaseless race for evolution and domination, the modern human becomes a vessel for the discarded.

Abundance examines how mass consumption corrodes not only our environments but the contours of identity itself. Through performance, sculpture, and ritual, it dwells on the moment where 'more' ceases to mean prosperity and begins to mean saturation.

The work explores the fragile equilibrium between individuality and the collective structures that contain it — a meditation on physical and emotional overload, on depersonalisation under the weight of excess.

Presented at Arte Laguna Prize, 20th Edition — Venice, 2025 ↗
