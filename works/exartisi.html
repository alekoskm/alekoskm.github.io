Εξάρτηση Dependency

Attachment, examined as structure — what supports us, and what slowly hollows us out.

Εξάρτηση turns the question of dependency into sculptural form. The piece dwells on the moment a need stops being need and becomes condition — when what once held the self in place begins, instead, to define its shape.

The work continues an ongoing inquiry into the body as a site of accumulation and erosion — the soft thresholds between care, compulsion, and collapse.

Presented at earth body — 2025
