Πολυκατοικία Apartment block

The architecture we share, and the architecture that contains us.

Πολυκατοικία takes the most ordinary form of Greek urban life — the apartment block — and reads it as a body. Concrete, vertical, densely shared, anonymous in its repetition: a building that is the everyday image of a collective that does not quite know itself.

The sculpture compresses this image into a single object — a study of how vertical density shapes the contour of the individual, and how communal walls translate, slowly, into private silences.
