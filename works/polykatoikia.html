Apartment Block Πολυκατοικία

Sculpture "Apartment Block"
Artist: Alexandros Mylonas, 2025
Materials: wood, metal, plaster bandage
Dimensions: 40cm × 35cm × 170cm (L × W × H)

A vertical structure. Stacked figures — masks of humans who have lost their identity. Where the housing crisis in megacities turns human beings into objects to be stored.

A structure like most buildings in densely populated areas, where existence suffocates.
